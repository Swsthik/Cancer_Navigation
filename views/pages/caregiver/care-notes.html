Care Documentation Tips
Be Specific and Objective

Focus on observable behaviors and symptoms. Include measurements when possible (temperature, BP, etc.) and avoid subjective judgments.

Document Timely

Record observations as soon as possible. This ensures accuracy and helps identify patterns or changes over time.

Share Meaningful Information

Focus on details that would be useful for the healthcare team, especially changes in condition, medication responses, and quality of life indicators.
